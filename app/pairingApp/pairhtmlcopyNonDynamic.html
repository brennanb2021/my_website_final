Grouping app

This program takes an excel sheet with a list of people, each with 3 other people who they donâ€™t want to be with and 3 people they do want to be with, in order.
It stops reading when it reads a row with both the first and last name columns blank.
The app then pairs the people based on their preferences.
It outputs 3 graphs: the first displays the complete graph, the second displays a graph with only nodes that have connections, and the third displays a graph containing the calculated best pairings.
A summary of the groupings will also appear on the left side of the page.
If you have a google sheets sheet, you can download it as an excel spreadsheet and use that.
